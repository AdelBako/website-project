Meet Era

It wasn’t until I started my health coach training program at the Institute of Integrative Nutrition (IIN) that I finally discovered what being healthy means to me. I struggled for years with disordered eating, insomnia, depression, anxiety, body shaming and never feeling enough despite my efforts to eat a nutritious diet and exercise regularly. As a student at IIN, I learned that health is so much more than food and exercise; it encompasses everything that nourishes the body on and off of the plate.

As I peeled back the layers of my health, I realized that being healthy is not about following a rigid diet or hitting my goal weight, it’s about listening to my body and treating myself with compassion. I slowly started letting go of my need to be perfect and allowed myself to indulge in all of my heart's desires. Lo and behold, I began to heal. I started eating what I wanted without shame, I started sleeping through the night, I started loving myself deeply and I started smiling brighter than ever.

My goal as a health coach is to support my clients in finding their own unique version of balance. I am here to offer positive encouragement and tips on how to live a wildly healthy life, filled with fun and free of rules.
